Magic Duel

Game Instructions

  1. Game is played between two players
  2. Users who have not registered must register and then log in
  3. After logging in, you can see the names of online users
  4. Click on the name of the user you want to play with
  5. After that, click on the character image to start the game
  6. During the game, each player has 3 lives
  7. Move your character using the keys: W (upward), A (left), S (downward), D (right)
  8. Collecting hearts will increase your lives by 1, while falling into traps will cause you to lose 1 life
  9. You can attack another player by clicking on their character
  10. When you are attacked, you will lose 1 life
  11. There is a cheat code (Only the inviting player can activate): By hitting the spacebar, your attack area will be doubled
  12. The player who first loses 3 lives loses the game
